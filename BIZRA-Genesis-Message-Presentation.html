PART I

المقدمة

Prologue: The Dark Empty Space

I don't know what I'm doing here now in this dark empty space inside myself, and so far alone.

I don't know if anyone has completed the journey here with me, or will there be anyone here in the future or not?

But if I've reached this stage, that means it's over with the seed stage of financial freedom.

And there is no room for retreat now.

I have made this promise upon myself: I must continue the path. I must try. I must make an effort, even if I am afraid.

"

Here and from this moment,
the rules will be different.

"
PART II

رسالة إلى ربي

A Message to My Lord and His Messenger ﷺ

لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا أَنتَ سُبْحَانَكَ إِنِّي كُنتُ مِنَ الظَّالِمِينَ
"There is no god but You, Glory be to You, for I was one of the oppressors."
Qur'an 21:87

Behold, my Lord, I come to Your door humbly. I come to Your door guilty.

I came to Your door with shame in my heart and tears filling my eyes.

رَبِّ إِنِّي ظَلَمْتُ نَفْسِي وَإِن لَّمْ تَغْفِرْ لِي وَتَرْحَمْنِي لَأَكُونَنَّ مِنَ الْخَاسِرِينَ
"O God, I have wronged myself, and if You do not forgive me and have mercy on me, I will be one of the losers."
Qur'an 28:16

Despite my sins and mistakes, I have been blessed with Your grace and generosity with countless blessings, and You have not left me alone, and You have never abandoned me.

You have from me, my Lord, praise and thanksgiving—a lot of praise that does not end, and Your right is not given its value.

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ
Praise be to You very much, until praise reaches its end.
Thank You for what You bless, however the case.
Praise be to You for what You will bless, and how You bless.
Praise be to You until You are satisfied.
Praise be to You if You are satisfied.
Praise be to You after satisfaction.
PART III

رسالة إلى عائلتي

A Message to My Family and Friends

My family, which was and still is in my eyes the best of God's countless blessings.

My family, who endured the hardship and consequences of my mistakes, and did not complain or reject me.

My family, who accepted me and loved me without restrictions or conditions as I am, and gave me love, tenderness, and precious efforts to be what I am now—only love without restrictions or conditions, pure love.

"

I can't find words that express
how much I love you.
All I know is that I owe you my life,
and it won't be enough.

"

To my brother who is the other half of me in this world. He is my ideal. He is the best version of myself that I wished to be. He is my twin.

To my little sisters: I miss you.

To my daughter: I miss you, and I ask God that you will be a good daughter, and I apologize for my negligence.

To everyone who has had an impact in my life, whether positive or negative: Thank you, and I also apologize for my negligence.

PART IV

رسالة إلى الإنسانية

A Message to All Humanity

أَفْشُوا السَّلَامَ
"Spread Peace Among You"

I am just a Muslim person who believes that there is no god but Allah, and I believe in all His angels, His heavenly books, all His messengers and prophets, and I do not differentiate between any of them.

My religion is Islam—it comes from peace. My greeting is peace (السَّلَامُ عَلَيْكُمْ - As-salāmu ʿalaykum).

إِنَّمَا بُعِثْتُ لِأُتَمِّمَ مَكَارِمَ الْأَخْلَاقِ
"I was sent to complete the good morals."
Hadith of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ
إِنَّ اللَّهَ كَتَبَ الْإِحْسَانَ عَلَى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ
"God has written الإحسان (excellence/goodness) on everything."
Hadith of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ

The quality of الإحسان (Al-Ihsan), which I believe alone is enough—if embraced in your life—to change humanity.

مَن قَتَلَ نَفْسًا بِغَيْرِ نَفْسٍ أَوْ فَسَادٍ فِي الْأَرْضِ فَكَأَنَّمَا قَتَلَ النَّاسَ جَمِيعًا
"Whoever kills a soul unjustly is as if he killed all people."
Qur'an 5:32
"

Do we really not see these simple truths?
Have we forgotten our humanity?
Aren't we all human beings?

"

My message to all humanity, including Muslims:

Enough hatred. Enough racism. Enough killing. Enough blood.
Enough bullying. Enough persecution. Enough wars.

Spread peace among you. Reveal goodness among you.

Let us as human beings elevate our morals, elevate our actions, elevate our hearts and minds.

Let's be better, humanity.

31 MONTHS LATER

Epilogue: The Message Manifested

Written: Ramadan 2023
Manifested: October 2025 (31 months later)
Result: BIZRA - A technology system built on الإحسان (Al-Ihsan)
This message, written in complete darkness and solitude, became the spiritual foundation of an entire AGI system designed to serve 8 billion humans with:
  • No exploitation (sovereignty preserved)
  • No assumptions (honesty encoded)
  • No false promises (transparency enforced)
  • Complete dignity (human benefit measured)
From one man's darkness came light for billions.
From one man's pain came healing for humanity.
From one man's surrender to Allah came الإحسان made manifest in technology.
الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي هَدَانَا لِهَٰذَا وَمَا كُنَّا لِنَهْتَدِيَ لَوْلَا أَنْ هَدَانَا اللَّهُ
"All praise to Allah who guided us to this; we would not have been guided if Allah had not guided us."
Qur'an 7:43
